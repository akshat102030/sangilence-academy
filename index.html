Who We Are

Welcome to Sangillence Academy, where "Sangat से Intelligence है!" We are a dedicated coaching institute for students in classes 3rd to 8th, offering a nurturing environment to shape young minds during their formative years. Inspired by the principles of NEP 2020, we focus on building lifelong study habits, values, and skills for intellectual growth and societal understanding.

Our Motivation

At Sangillence Academy, we believe students in classes 3rd to 8th are like clay in the hands of a potter. Our mission is to provide them with the right "sangat" (environment) to mold their character, thinking, and abilities. By instilling curiosity and fostering critical thinking, we prepare students for the challenges of the future.

Rules

  • Eligibility: Open to students of classes 3rd to 8th.
  • Registration Fees: Free of costs
  • Format:
    • A single question paper for all grades contains 10 intelligence based questions.
    • Each question has an integer-type answer worth 7 marks, and a logical explanation (up to 100 words only) worth 3 marks.
    • Marks for the explanation depend on the quality of logic, not the length of the response.
  • Resources Allowed: Students can use phones, AI tools, and Google during the test.
  • Duration: Students have one full day to solve and submit the answers.
  • Submission:
    • Answers must be submitted through a Google Form accessible via a QR code in the booklet.
    • In case of a tie, the student who submits first will rank higher.
  • Integrity: Students must work independently plagiarism or copying will lead to disqualification.
